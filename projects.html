Projects
Education and Training

we believe that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation. Our Education and Training initiatives aim to equip individuals—especially children, youth, and women—with the knowledge and skills they need to break the cycle of poverty, achieve their potential, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Health and Well being

At Muslim Family Counseling Service (MFCS), we are deeply committed to promoting holistic health and well-being within our communities. Guided by our faith-based values and a commitment to compassion, dignity, and equity, our health initiatives focus on improving the physical, mental, and emotional wellness of individuals and families—especially those who are vulnerable or underserved.

Community Water System

access to clean, safe water is not only a basic human need—but a right that upholds health, dignity, and justice. Our Community Water Systems initiative is part of our commitment to improving the well-being of vulnerable families by ensuring reliable access to clean water for drinking, cooking, sanitation, and hygiene.

Clothing and Food Donations

every individual deserves dignity, warmth, and nourishment—regardless of their circumstances. Our Clothing and Food Donation Program is rooted in compassion and the Islamic principle of caring for those in need

Health Education

At Muslim Family Counseling Services (MFCS), we believe that access to accurate, culturally sensitive health information is a vital step toward building healthier individuals, families, and communities. Our Health Education Program empowers people with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their physical, mental, and emotional well-being—rooted in both medical best practices and Islamic values.

Charity Work

our charity work is rooted in the Islamic values of compassion, justice, and community care. Guided by the principles of sadaqah, zakat, and rahma (mercy), we strive to uplift those facing hardship by offering direct assistance, ongoing support, and hope for a better future.

Food Donation

Nourishing Bodies. Uplifting Spirits. Our Food Donation Program is a vital part of our mission to serve vulnerable individuals and families with dignity and compassion. Rooted in the Islamic values of charity (sadaqah) and community care, we strive to ease the burden of food insecurity by delivering nutritious meals and grocery support where it’s needed most.

Signage Donation to Promote Security

As part of our ongoing commitment to community development and collaboration, Muslim Family Counseling Services (MFCS) proudly donated a new directional and identification signage to the Asewase Police Station. This initiative reflects our belief that strong, safe communities are built through meaningful partnerships and visible support for public institutions.

Productive Workshops

Our Productive Workshops are designed to equip individuals—especially women, youth, and underserved community members—with practical skills, confidence, and the tools they need to thrive socially and economically.

Reproductive Health

promoting safe, healthy, and informed lives for women, men, and families in our community. Through our Reproductive Health and Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Programs, we provide education, support, and resources that honor our Islamic values

Volunteer and Training

Serve with Purpose. Grow with Compassion. At Muslim Family Services, we believe that true change begins with people who care. Our Volunteer and Training Program offers a meaningful opportunity for individuals to give back to the community while gaining valuable skills, knowledge, and experience.

Free Health Screenings

At Muslim Family Counseling Services, we are committed to supporting the well-being of individuals and families within the Asewase community. Our health screening services are designed to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle and prevent potential health issues before they become more serious. Regular health screenings are a key component of overall health maintenance, and we are here to guide you through this essential process.
